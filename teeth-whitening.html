Frequently Asked Questions

Is teeth whitening safe?

Yes, professional teeth whitening is completely safe when performed by our experienced dental professionals using FDA-approved products.

How long do results last?

With proper care, teeth whitening results can last 1-3 years. We provide aftercare instructions and maintenance products to help extend your results.

Will I experience sensitivity?

Some patients may experience mild sensitivity during or after treatment. We use desensitizing agents and can adjust the treatment to minimize discomfort.

How many shades whiter will my teeth get?

Most patients see their teeth become 3-8 shades whiter, depending on the starting shade and the package chosen. We'll discuss realistic expectations during your consultation.
