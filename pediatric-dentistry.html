Tips for Parents

Help prepare your child for a positive dental experience.

Before the Visit

  • • Read dental books together
  • • Practice opening mouth wide
  • • Avoid using scary words
  • • Schedule at a good time for your child
  • • Arrive early to get familiar with the office

During the Visit

  • • Stay positive and calm
  • • Let the dental team lead
  • • Praise your child's cooperation
  • • Ask questions if needed
  • • Follow the dentist's guidance
