Benefits of Orthodontic Treatment

Orthodontic treatment offers more than just aesthetic improvements – it's an investment in your overall oral health and well-being.

Improved Oral Health

Straight teeth are easier to clean, reducing risk of cavities and gum disease.

Better Bite Function

Properly aligned teeth improve chewing efficiency and reduce jaw strain.

Enhanced Confidence

A beautiful smile boosts self-esteem and social confidence.

Long-term Benefits

Prevents future dental problems and preserves teeth for life.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does orthodontic treatment take?

Treatment typically takes 12-24 months, depending on the complexity of your case and the type of appliance used.

Do braces hurt?

You may experience some discomfort when braces are first placed and after adjustments, but this typically subsides within a few days.

Can I eat normally with braces?

Yes, though you'll need to avoid hard, sticky, or very crunchy foods. We'll provide a complete list of foods to avoid.

How often are adjustment appointments?

Typically every 4-8 weeks, depending on your treatment plan and the type of braces you have.
