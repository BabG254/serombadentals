Understanding Gum Disease

Gum disease affects millions of people worldwide and is a leading cause of tooth loss in adults.

Gingivitis

Early stage gum disease with inflammation, bleeding, and swelling of gums.

Periodontitis

Advanced gum disease affecting the bones and tissues supporting teeth.

Advanced Periodontitis

Severe bone loss and tooth mobility that may require tooth extraction.

Warning Signs of Gum Disease

Early detection is key to successful treatment. Watch for these symptoms.

Bleeding Gums

Gums that bleed during brushing or flossing

Swollen Gums

Red, swollen, or tender gums

Bad Breath

Persistent bad breath or bad taste

Loose Teeth

Teeth that feel loose or are shifting

Prevention is Key

The best treatment for gum disease is prevention. Follow these simple steps to maintain healthy gums.

Daily Brushing & Flossing

Brush twice daily and floss once to remove plaque buildup.

Regular Dental Cleanings

Professional cleanings every 6 months to remove tartar.

Healthy Lifestyle

Don't smoke, eat a balanced diet, and manage stress.
