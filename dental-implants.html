The Implant Process

A step-by-step approach to ensure successful implant placement and healing.

1

Consultation & Planning

Comprehensive exam, 3D imaging, and treatment planning.

2

Implant Placement

Surgical placement of titanium implant into the jawbone.

3

Healing Period

3-6 months for implant to integrate with bone (osseointegration).

4

Crown Placement

Custom crown attachment for a complete, natural-looking tooth.

Why Choose Dental Implants?

Lifetime Solution

With proper care, implants can last a lifetime, making them a cost-effective long-term solution.

Eat Anything

Enjoy all your favorite foods without restrictions or worry about slipping dentures.

Protect Other Teeth

Unlike bridges, implants don't require grinding down adjacent healthy teeth.

Confidence Boost

Smile, speak, and laugh with complete confidence knowing your teeth look and feel natural.

Preserve Bone

Stimulate jawbone like natural tooth roots, preventing bone loss and facial collapse.

Easy Maintenance

Care for implants just like natural teeth with regular brushing and flossing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Am I a candidate for dental implants?

Most people with good general health and adequate jawbone density are candidates. We'll evaluate your specific situation during consultation.

How long does the process take?

The complete process typically takes 3-6 months, allowing time for the implant to integrate with your jawbone before placing the crown.

Is the procedure painful?

Most patients report minimal discomfort. We use local anesthesia during the procedure and provide pain management instructions for recovery.

What is the success rate?

Dental implants have a success rate of over 95% when placed by experienced professionals and properly maintained.
